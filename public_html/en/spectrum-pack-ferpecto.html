Pack Ferpecto

Compilation that includes some classic Compiler Software titles in DSK format.

Thanks to this format, it will be easy to transfer games to a physical support, either to a 3 or 3.5 inches disk or to a Compact Flash card, in order to play on the real hardware.

You will be able to select the game you want to play through a friendly menu.

This is Camelot Warriors font, indeed

Games included are:

  • Another brick on the wall 2.
  • ZX Columns.
  • ZX Mines.
  • ZX Mines 2.
  • ZX Poker.
